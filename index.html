01 — About

Who I am

I'm currently taking Bachelor in Data Science at the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre (EASTC) in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, working across the full data stack — from cleaning raw CSVs to shipping models people actually use.

Most of my recent work sits at the intersection of machine learning and public-interest data: classifying health-risk records, analysing data to build insights, and building lightweight tools for problems specific to the Tanzanian context — like a phone-theft alert concept built on SMS infrastructure.

I move comfortably between Python, R, SQL,Excel and Power BI, and I care about work that's reproducible: clean pipelines, documented workflows, and reports that a non-technical reader can still follow.
